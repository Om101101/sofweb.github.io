Rajasthani Thali

"Rajasthani cuisine is the cuisine of the rugged Rajasthan region in North West India. It was influenced by both the warlike lifestyles of its inhabitants and the availability of ingredients in an arid region. Food that could last for several days and could be eaten without heating was preferred."

Punjabi Thali

"This thali is a lot like the people in Punjab - robust and full of life. The popular Pujabi thali consists of Amritsari Kulcha or Naan, Pindhi Chhole, Dal Makhani, Jeera Rice, Paneer Makhani and a big glass of Lassi. The non-vegetarian thali may include Butter Chicken, Amritsari Machchi and Naan."
